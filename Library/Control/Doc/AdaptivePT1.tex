V & Gain\tabularnewline
\hline
fmax & Maximum frequency [Hz]

\hline

\hline
method & Discretization method\tabularnewline
\hline
\end{XtoCtabular}

\subsubsection*{Description:}
First order low pass with adaptive cut off frequency:

    G(s) = V/(s/(2*pi*fc) + 1)
